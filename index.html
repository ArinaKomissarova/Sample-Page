Here is some infromation about Karl Terzaghi

  • Known as the father of Soil Mechanics


  • Terzaghi won many honors, including the Norman Medal of the American Society of Civil Engineers in 1930, 1943, 1946, and 1955.


  • Terzaghi's writings contain significant contributions on many topics, especially consolidation theory, foundation design and construction, cofferdam analysis, and landslide mechanisms.


  • Terzaghi was given nine honorary doctorate degrees coming from universities in eight different countries.
